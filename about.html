What is AlgoScreen Pro?

AlgoScreen Pro is a free stock screener and market dashboard built specifically for swing traders — people who hold positions for days to weeks, not hours or years.

It combines real-time market data, technical analysis, macro context, and curated news into a single interface. No login required. No subscription. No ads.

How the AlgoScore works

Every stock is scored from 0–100. The score starts at 50 and points are added or subtracted based on 9 factors. Higher score = more bullish signals aligned.
