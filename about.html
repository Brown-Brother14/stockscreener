What is AlgoScreen Pro?

AlgoScreen Pro is a free stock screener and market dashboard built for swing traders — people who hold positions for days to weeks, not hours or years.

It combines real-time market data, technical analysis, macro context, and market news into a single interface. No login, no subscription, no ads.

How the AlgoScore works

Every stock gets scored from 0–100. The score starts at 50 and points are added or subtracted based on 9 factors. A higher score means more bullish signals are aligned.
