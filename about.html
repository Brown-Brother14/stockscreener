The product

What is AlgoScreen Pro?

AlgoScreen Pro is a market screener built for swing traders. It pulls live data from multiple sources, runs a scoring algorithm across 9 technical and fundamental factors, and surfaces the clearest setups — all in one place.

No subscriptions · No login · No paywall

Swing trading is about catching moves over days or weeks. AlgoScreen focuses on setups where multiple signals agree simultaneously — RSI in the bullish zone, EMA trend pointing up, ADX confirming a strong trend.

The AlgoScore condenses all of that into a single 0–100 number so you can rank 500 stocks in seconds, not hours.

How the AlgoScore works

Every stock starts at 50. Points are added or subtracted based on 9 factors. The higher the score, the more bullish signals are aligned right now.
